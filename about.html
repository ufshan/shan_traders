Our Story

Established in 2021, Shan Traders began with a simple mission: to provide high-quality construction materials at honest prices. Located on the bustling Tirur-Chemravettom Road in Perinthaloor, we've grown into a trusted name among contractors, builders, and homeowners.

We believe that strong buildings start with strong materials. That's why we partner only with reputed brands and ensure every product we sell meets rigorous quality standards.
